# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to a welcoming, respectful, and harassment-free community for everyone.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Assume positive intent and focus on technical outcomes.
- Provide actionable feedback grounded in evidence.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks.
- Publishing private information without permission.
- Disruptive conduct that prevents productive collaboration.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing this code of conduct and may take appropriate corrective action in response to unacceptable behavior.
